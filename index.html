Our Process

We connect buyers with verified sellers and provide end-to-end document support including NCNDA, IMFPA, LOI, SCO, and SPA. We support CIF, FOB, and spot/term transactions globally.

  1. Buyers & Sellers submit a Pre-Qualification Form (provided by Enerquo)
  2. Verification and Onboarding by Enerquo after receiving forms and documents (15-day turnaround)
  3. Document exchange (LOI, SCO, NCNDA, IMFPA)
  4. Facilitation of deal between matched parties
  5. Ongoing support until delivery & closure
  6. Contract renewals
