About Enerquo

I am the Founder and Managing Director of Enerquo, an independent oil and energy brokerage specializing in connecting verified petroleum suppliers with high-demand markets across Asia, Africa, and other emerging regions.

At Enerquo, I prioritize transparency, long-term partnerships, and eco-conscious trading practices. I work directly with oil producers, refineries, and energy trading companies to facilitate smooth, secure, and compliant international transactions.

Services

  • Sourcing credible oil producers, refineries, and suppliers
  • Facilitating transactions between verified buyers and sellers
  • Supporting compliance documents:
    • NCNDA
    • IMFPA
    • LOI, SCO, SPA
  • Legal protections for agents & intermediaries
  • CIF, FOB, spot & term contracts

Global Markets

  • Southeast Asia
  • Sub-Saharan Africa
  • Latin America
  • Middle East

Our 6-Step Process

  1. Pre-Qualification: Submit form & documentation
  2. Onboarding: Due diligence (15 days)
  3. Document Exchange: LOI, SCO, NCNDA, IMFPA
  4. Deal Structuring: Matchmaking & negotiation
  5. Support: Transaction monitoring
  6. Renewals: Extend contracts as needed
